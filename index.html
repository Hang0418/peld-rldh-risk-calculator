2-Year RLDH Risk Calculator After PELD

A research-use calculator for estimating 2-year recurrent lumbar disc herniation risk after percutaneous endoscopic lumbar discectomy.

This tool is intended for research-use risk communication and follow-up stratification. It should not be used as a stand-alone treatment-decision instrument.

Suggested interpretation

Enter patient variables and calculate risk. The output will summarize the predicted probability and risk stratum.

Research-use disclaimer

The predicted probability is intended to support individualized risk communication and postoperative follow-up stratification. It should not be used as a stand-alone indication for revision surgery, fusion surgery, or treatment escalation.

This result should be interpreted together with clinical symptoms, neurological findings, imaging results, segmental stability, surgeon judgment, and patient preference.

Model information

The calculator is based on the locked full prespecified logistic-regression model refit on the complete training cohort. XGBoost was used separately for machine-learning model-level interpretation in the study.

Continuous variables are entered in their original clinical units. The validation cohort was not used for predictor selection, threshold selection, or coefficient estimation for the deployment model.
